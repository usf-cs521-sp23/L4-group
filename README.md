# Wordle!

See here: https://www.cs.usfca.edu/~mmalensek/cs521/assignments/wordle.html

1. Enter your name

2.choose the word size you want to play with

3.Enter a word of size of your choice

4.You have 6 chances

5.Whether you win or lose enter y to play again

6.After you feel satisfied, there is a results.txt that record the records that you have played

7.Also a stats.txt that records the total score of every palyer and the rank

8.Now you can show off to those who falls behind you!!!

9.Have fun!!!
